Elements Project

Brief

This project was to create a website to teach either for a postgraduate student or for a student in the 10-12 age range. I quickly decided i wanted to go for a younger audience because I figured I would be able to add more illustrations to the site.

This is just a quick sketch of my first ideas when it came to creating the website, there is a quick design for undergrads and the primary school student.

The primary school design is based on playing cards with one side being an illustration and the flip side having a short piece of information on the element.

My theme was on the elements that are in the medicinal treatment of radiotherapy which gave me five elements; Uranium, Radon, Radium, Plutonium and Cobalt.

Here is a few designs that I made for each element.

Making these sketches into the illustrator was the fun part. After deciding the final design I got stuck into illustrator and came up with these!
